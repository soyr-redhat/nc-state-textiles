# Q2 2024 Sales Performance Report
**Reporting Period:** April 1 – June 30, 2024
**Prepared by:** Sales Operations Team
**Date:** July 15, 2024

---

## Executive Summary

Q2 2024 showed strong performance across most product categories, with total revenue of **$1.87M**, representing **18% growth** compared to Q1 2024 and **23% year-over-year growth** versus Q2 2023.

**Key Highlights:**
- Activewear and summer categories drove significant growth
- West region outperformed all other regions by 34%
- Accessories category showed unexpected strength
- Footwear remained consistently strong across all demographics

### Analysis:

- **Outerwear (-20%):** Expected seasonal decline as temperatures rose
- **Tops (-16%):** Sweaters and cardigans down (seasonal); summer tops not compensating
- **Bottoms (-12%):** Jeans underperforming; customer feedback cites fit issues

## Product Quality & Customer Feedback

### Customer Satisfaction Trends

**Positive Themes:**
1. Premium product quality exceeding expectations
2. Functional design (especially bags and activewear)
3. Accurate online representation
4. Fast shipping and customer service

**Negative Themes:**
1. **Fit inconsistencies** (jeans, boots) – most common complaint
2. **Budget line quality issues** (pilling, color fading, durability)
3. **Sizing inaccuracy** – "runs small" or "runs large" confusion
4. **Product photos misleading on size** (handbags)

### Recommended Actions:

1. **Immediate:** Conduct fit audit on denim and footwear lines
2. **Short-term:** Improve product photography with size references
3. **Medium-term:** Review budget line suppliers; improve QC or discontinue low performers
4. **Long-term:** Implement customer fit preference system

## Pricing & Promotion Analysis

### Promotional Impact

- **Discounted Products:** 23% of total units sold
- **Average Discount:** 18%
- **Margin Impact:** -4.2% overall margin compression

**Best Performing Promotions:**
1. "Buy 2 Get 15% Off" (accessories) – drove 34% lift
2. "Summer Dress Sale" – moved excess inventory effectively
3. "New Customer 10% Off" – strong acquisition tool

**Underperforming Promotions:**
1. "BOGO 50% Off Activewear" – cannibalized full-price sales
2. "Clearance Sweaters" – minimal uptake (wrong timing)

**Recommendation:** Reduce broad discounting; focus on bundle deals and new customer acquisition.

## Conclusion

Q2 2024 demonstrated strong revenue growth driven by strategic category strength (activewear, dresses, accessories) and regional momentum (West, Southeast). However, operational challenges around inventory balance, product quality in budget lines, and regional distribution require immediate attention.

**Critical Actions for Q3:**
1. Rebalance regional inventory (shift Midwest → West)
2. Address fit and quality issues (denim, budget line)
3. Expand activewear to meet demand
4. Clear outerwear overstock proactively
5. Optimize promotional strategy to protect margins

With focused execution on these priorities, Q3 is positioned to exceed Q2 performance and set up a strong holiday season in Q4.
